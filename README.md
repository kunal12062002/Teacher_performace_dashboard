## Dashboard Pages

| Page | What it shows |
|------|--------------|
| **Staff Dashboard** | School-wide KPIs, monthly trend, section charts, top/bottom 5 teachers, attrition distribution |
| **Teacher Profile** | Individual deep-dive — gauge, compliance, CCA, stakeholder ratings, HOD expectations, PDF download |
| **Punctuality & Retention** | Per-teacher late history, school-wide late trends, attrition risk table with CSV export |

## Scoring Formula

```
Overall Score = 60% Teaching Quality + 25% Admin Compliance + 15% CCA
```

## Dataset Summary (generated by `dataset.py`)

| Table | Rows | Key Columns |
|-------|------|-------------|
| Teacher Master | 20 | teacher_id, name, subject, section, qualification, experience, duties |
| Performance Monthly | 240 | month, teaching_delivery_bm, teaching_delivery_curr, compliance_score, cca_score |
| Late Arrivals | 240 | month, late_count (May = 0, summer break) |
| Attrition Risk | 20 | attrition_score (0–3), risk_level, exit_type, explanation |
| Compliance | 20 | training_hours (out of 50), assignment_correction_stars (1–5) |
| CCA | 20 | activities_completed, activities_total, quality |
| Stakeholder Ratings | 80 | stakeholder (Head/Peer/Student/Parent), stars (1–5) |

---

## Key Design Decisions

- **Month filter** in sidebar applies globally across all 3 pages
- **Attrition risk** is an annual snapshot — intentionally does not change by month
- **Compliance & CCA** are year-to-date figures — static per teacher
- **May = 0 late arrivals** — summer break, school not in session
- **Benchmark score** has small random noise only (±3), not a rising trend
- **Actual score** has a seasonal sine boost peaking Sep–Nov (exam prep cycle)
- **Academic Year** runs Apr 2025 – Mar 2026 (Indian school calendar)
